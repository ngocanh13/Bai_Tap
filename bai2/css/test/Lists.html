Scrambled Eggs

Egg are one of my favorite foods. Here is a recipe for deliciously rich scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan over a mediun heat
  2. Gently mix the eggs and crean in a bowl
  3. Once butter has melted add cream and eggs
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center every 20 seconds (as if you making an omelette)
  5. when the eggs still moist remove from the heat (it will coninue to cook on the plate until served)
